Sustainable Solar Lamp

About

A solar-powered lamp intended for placement on an island without access to an electric grid, ensuring a self-sustaining power source.

Mechanical Setup

  • LED Lamp: includes 10 dimmable 3W LEDs connected in parallel and the Light Sensor consisting of the Arduino UNO and the Light Dependent Resistor
  • Solar Panel: 315-325 W Power Output Range, and a maximum efficiency of 19.48%
  • Battery: consists of rechargeable 12V battery with a capacity of 120 mAh

Circuit Setup

Modes of Failure

  1. Heat Generation: Potential battery damage due to heat generated by components
  2. Environmental Impact on PV Efficiency: Output efficiency could be affected by shading/soiling due to external environmental conditions
  3. Exposure to UV rays: Cracks and degradation of components (especially the panels) due to continuous exposure to UV rays
